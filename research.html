Research

My research is in commutative algebra and its interactions with combinatorics and algebraic geometry. In particular, I am interested in multiplicities, blowup algebras, integral dependence, residual intersections, local cohomology, symbolic powers, resolutions, and convex geometry.


My research is supported by NSF Grant DMS #2001645.
