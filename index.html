CV

My research is supported by NSF Grant DMS #2001645/2303605.

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at Arizona State University.

Until the summer of 2022, I was an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at New Mexico State University. I was granted tenure and promoted to Associate Professor in the spring of 2022.

Previously, I was a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at University of Kansas.

Before joining KU, I was a Ph.D. student at Purdue University working under the direction of Professor Bernd Ulrich.
